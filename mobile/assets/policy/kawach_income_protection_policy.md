# Kawach Income Protection Policy

## Terms and Conditions

Version 1.0 | Effective: April 2026  
Issued by: Kawach InsurTech Platform | Powered by licensed insurer partner to be confirmed

## Important Notice

Please read this document before purchasing a policy. By completing payment, you confirm that you understand and agree to the terms below.

This is a parametric income protection product. It does not cover health, vehicle damage, accidents, or general liability. Coverage is limited to verified income loss caused by qualifying disruption events in your registered delivery zone.

## 1. Definitions

- Kawach or Platform: the mobile platform that distributes and manages this policy.
- Insurer: the licensed insurer underwriting the risk.
- Policyholder or Rider: a registered delivery partner who has purchased a valid policy.
- Policy: one weekly income protection contract with a 7-day coverage period.
- Primary Delivery Zone: the rider's registered working zone.
- Active Work Session: a logged-in work period with location/session validation enabled.

## 2. Eligibility

You may purchase a policy only if you are:

- 18 years or older
- Active on a supported delivery platform
- KYC verified on the app
- Operating in the supported Bengaluru zone grid
- Not already holding another active Kawach policy for the same coverage period

## 3. Coverage Scope

This policy covers verified loss of income caused by qualifying disruption events in your registered zone during an active work session.

Covered events include:

- Heavy rainfall
- Urban flooding
- Extreme heat
- Severe AQI
- Severe thunderstorm

Coverage applies only when the system confirms the threshold conditions for the event.

## 4. Trigger Logic

The app uses environmental thresholds and session signals to determine whether a payout is due.

Example thresholds used by the app:

- Heavy Rainfall: 50mm or more in 3 hours
- Urban Flooding: 80mm or more plus flood-prone zone flag
- Extreme Heat: 43C or more for 2 consecutive hours
- Severe AQI: 300 or more for 6 consecutive hours
- Thunderstorm: 60 km/h wind or higher with active storm alert

## 5. Policy Period and Waiting Period

- Each policy covers 7 calendar days after activation.
- A waiting period of 12 to 24 hours may apply after payment.
- Events that begin during the waiting period are not covered.
- The policy does not auto-renew.

## 6. Payout Rules

- Payouts are calculated from disruption severity, activity drop, and income deviation.
- The app may trigger a payout even if your personal loss is smaller than the payout amount.
- The app will not pay if environmental thresholds are not met.
- Coverage caps and payout caps apply.

## 7. Exclusions

This policy does not cover:

- Health claims
- Vehicle damage or maintenance
- General accidents
- Theft
- Liability claims
- Events outside the registered delivery zone
- Events during the waiting period

## 8. Cancellation

- Cancellation during the waiting period: full refund minus a processing fee
- Cancellation within 24 hours of activation: partial refund if no disruption event was triggered
- Cancellation after 24 hours of active coverage: no refund

## 9. Fraud and Misrepresentation

The insurer or platform may reject, reduce, delay, or reverse payouts if fraud, misrepresentation, or abuse is detected.

## 10. Data Use

Rider data may be used for eligibility checks, payout calculation, fraud detection, and model improvement. Data is not sold to third parties.
